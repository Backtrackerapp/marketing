Our Vision

Plot, Track, Share

Plot your journey. Track your friends. Share your stories.

Today, a trip can be shared a thousand ways online, but it’s easily lost in the vast ocean of social media and internet blogs. BackTracker is a new, interactive space for you to save all your journeys and memories and share them with your friends and family.

Highlights

Looking for that hostel you could happily call home? That bar that only the locals know? That quirky café to sit and watch the world go by?

BackTracker only recommends the best. With a team of travel-hardened backpackers and a wealth of local knowledge, we’re here to provide you with a concise and current catalogue of your destination’s best kept secrets. Inside knowledge at the click of the button.

Friends

Isabel, that girl you met in London. Marco, that guy from your hostel in Venice. Facebook photos show they were in Berlin at the same time as you. How many other people have you missed?

Backtracker shows you the future before it happens. Let the friends you’ve made know where you are and where you’ll be, not where you were. This way you can arrange to meet up or, if they’re one step ahead, contact them for tips and up-to-date info.

Plot, track and share with the people you meet.

Privacy Policy

We are committed to ensuring and protecting your privacy any time you are registered to the app and via your Facebook or Twitter account. Our privacy policy details how we may use your personal information, provided to us legally.

Updates will be frequent, so please refer back to this policy once an update is completed.

  1. Information collected from you

    These methods of collection are approved:

    • If you sign up via your email account, we can collect the information provided.
    • If you sign up via Facebook, we can collect the information made available to us, which will be made clear during the sign-up process
    • If you the BackTracker App we may collect all communications with the BackTracker Hub (e.g. designations, ratings, follows, journeys) and usage logs.
    • If you visit BackTracker’s website, we may collect traffic data, web-logs and any other communication with BackTracker’s website

  2. Use of your information

    Information stored and collected about you allows us to improve our platform, apps, or website for you.

    We may use your information to provide you updates or notifications about the BackTracker app.

    We will ask for your approval before using information for a purpose other than those that are set out in this Privacy Policy.

  3. Storing and processing your personal data

    We process personal information on our servers in many countries around the world. We may process your personal information on a server located outside the country where you live or outside the European Union if you live in the European Union. We take all reasonable processes to ensure your data is secure and in agreement with our Privacy Policy as outlined here.

We do not and cannot guarantee security of information collected electronically or transmitted; however, we take all necessary steps to provide the best security available. As a result of our inability to guarantee safety, you are submitting information to us at your own risk. Where needed a password may be necessary to access areas of our apps or website. You are responsible for the safety and confidentiality of the password you generate.
