BackTracker

Your blog, your guide, your community.

The app for backpackers, by backpackers.

Available online and for iPad and iPhone.

Journey

Plot your journey on our detailed map-based interface as you travel, posting updates and photos of your trip at each pinned location. BackTracker then joins the dots to create a trail.

BackTracker lets you keep your journey private, or share it with family and friends (via our interactive website, Facebook or Twitter). You can also leave your profile open, build up followers, and share your trips with the whole BackTracker community.

Friends

BackTracker brings you your very own travelling social network to stay in contact with the people you meet. Connect through Facebook and follow users with open profiles on the map. A pin will show a user’s most recently updated location.

Use the chat feature to contact another user. It may be that they’re in a place you’re heading to, so get hold of them for tips and advice. You can also add a ‘ghost’ pin to show your next destination, making meeting up that much easier.

Highlights

Dotted on our map, you will find a selection of highly recommended, cool and different hostels, bars, restaurants and travel tips that are aimed at young backpackers on a budget, looking to have fun and meet other travellers.

Our highlights map is open to the BackTracker community, so send in your personal recommendations and participate in enhancing everyone’s travelling experience! That way we can keep all the information up-to-date and ensure that nothing worthwhile gets missed.
